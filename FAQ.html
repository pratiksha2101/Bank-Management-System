4. How do I access Online STUDENTS BANK OF SPARK?

You need to have an account at a branch. You also need to register for the Internet banking service with the branch.
Branch will provide you a Pre Printed Kit (PPK) containing username and password for first login.
If you are not in a position to collect PPK in person, the bank will arrange to send a username through SMS and a mailer containing password to your registered address.
Log On to www.studentsbankofspark.com using this username and password.
At the first login, you will need to go through a simple initialization process.
Our Net banking assistant will guide you step by step through this process on the site.
